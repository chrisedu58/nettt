Stranger Things

"Stranger Things," a Netflix sci-fi horror-drama, follows a group of young friends in 1980s Hawkins, Indiana, as they uncover supernatural mysteries after a boy named Will Byers mysteriously disappears. The town is rocked by strange events involving a secret government laboratory, a portal to an alternate dimension called the Upside Down, and terrifying creatures, while the children and their parents, including Joyce and Police Chief Hopper, must confront these forces to find Will and protect their community.
